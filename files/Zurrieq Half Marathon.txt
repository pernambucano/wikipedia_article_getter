Zurrieq Half Marathon
The Zurrieq Half Marathon is a road running event held every November in Malta, Europe. The race starts from the Zurrieq Main Square and finishes in the same location after passing through the picturesque towns of Safi, Kirkop, Mqabba, Qrendi and Siggiewi.


Course records
Men - Christian Nemeth (Belgian) - 1:09:36 in 2006
Women - Carol Galea (Malta) - 1:20:37 in 2004
