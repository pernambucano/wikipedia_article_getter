Campagne (restaurant)
Campagne (French pronunciation: ​[kɑ̃.paɲ], meaning "countryside") is a restaurant in Kilkenny, Ireland. It is a fine dining restaurant that was awarded a Michelin star for 2014 and 2015.
Head chef Gareth Byrne, formerly of Chapter One, opened the restaurant in 2008.


AwardsEdit
Michelin star: 2013
Georgina Campbell's Restaurant of the Year Award: 2010.
