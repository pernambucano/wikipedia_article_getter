Balkan cuisine
Balkan cuisine is heavily influenced by Eastern European and Mediterranean cooking.
The cuisine of the Balkan states includes:
Albanian cuisine
Bosnia and Herzegovina cuisine
Bulgarian cuisine
Croatian cuisine
Greek cuisine
Macedonian cuisine (Greek)

Macedonian cuisine
Montenegrin cuisine
Ottoman cuisine
Romanian cuisine
Serbian cuisine
Turkish cuisine
